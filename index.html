Weeks 6-10 | 8 Activities

Comprehensive review of existing IMRS, standards mapping, workshops with element owners, and maturity assessment. Key milestone: M2 - Diagnostic Validation Workshop

D4: Gap Assessment Report D5: Executive Summary Deck

Weeks 11-14 | 6 Activities

Design integrated governance architecture, map cross-element controls, develop visual governance model. Key milestone: M3 - Architecture Approval

D6: Integrated Architecture Map D7: Cross-Element Control Matrix

Phase 3: Updated IMRS Framework & Documentation

Weeks 15-18 | 8 Activities

Develop updated IMRS master document, control library, standard crosswalk annexes, and implementation guidance. Key milestone: M4 - IMRS Framework Approval

D8: Updated IMRS Master Document D9: Crosswalk & Control Library Pack

Phase 4: Digital Transformation of IMRS

Weeks 19-22 | 7 Activities

Design and deploy digital IMRS platform with workflows, dashboards, and role-based access. Key milestones: M5 - Architecture Selection, M6 - Digital Go-Live

D10: Architecture Selection Memo D11: Functional Requirement Spec D12: Digital IMRS Portal Live

Phase 5: Branding & Identity Refresh

Weeks 23-25 | 5 Activities

Develop naming options, logo concepts, brand identity pack, and update all templates. Key milestone: M7 - Naming Approval

D13: Naming Memo D14: Brand Identity Pack D15: Updated Templates

Phase 6: Audit Framework, Training & Institutionalization

Weeks 26-30 | 8 Activities

Develop audit framework, training materials, conduct role-based training, and institutionalize governance. Key milestone: M8 - Project Closure & Institutionalization
